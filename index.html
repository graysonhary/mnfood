Minnesota Food

Having spent a few years in the Twin Cities, I have found myself enjoying some wonderful meals with wonderful people. All over the metro, there's something new cooking, just waiting to be found. This website, I hope, can be a resource to friends, new and old, in discovering the culinary delight that the Twin Cities, and the rest of Minnesota, have to offer.

Minneapolis — Northeast

Young Joni

165 13th Ave NE, Minneapolis, MN 55413

One of the best spots for pizza, salads, and cocktails in Minneapolis! I love salads and their salads WAIL!!!

Chimborazo

2851 Central Ave NE, Minneapolis, MN 55418

Stellar Ecuadorian fare in Northeast Minneapolis. A quiet and quaint, warm environment. The BEST fried plaintains in town, in addition to a small but exemplary menu offering a variety of delicious options. Staff is baller and super down to Earth!

The Sample Room

2124 Marshall St NE, Minneapolis, MN 55418

An inconspicuous little building lies off Marshall street, not too far South of Lowry Avenue, nor too far from the ever popular Psycho Suzis. This little building is home to The Sample Room, one of, in my opinion, the less talked about spots in Northeast (or Minneapolis, for that matter). Playing host to a great bar program, and an even better food program, the burger (named after French explore, Pierre Bottineau), and their Cuban, are two of the best sandwiches available in town (in addition to what may be the best fries in town, too)!

Saint Paul

Revival

525 Selby Ave, St Paul, MN 55102

Serving up excellent fried chicken, and a myriad of other Southern comfort food, Revival stands out in the Twin Cities for the experience they deliver. On top of their chicken, they have a great beef brisket (the sandwich being my favorite). Though there are two Revival locations, the original being in South Minneapolis, the one in Saint Paul has a full bar. If you've got room after dinner, stick around for a scrumptious piece of pie!

Mucci's Italian

786 Randolph Ave, St Paul, MN 55102

A fantastic little Italian spot in Saint Paul, tucked between 35E and West Seventh, on Randolph Avenue. The tiramasu is a must (always, duh), and I highly recommend the three course dinner!!! Fantastic staff, who are always upbeat and make it readily apparent that they love what they do!

Brooklyn Park

Lemongrass Thai

8600 Edinburgh Center Dr, Minneapolis, MN 55443

A great Thai spot that also features great sushi offerings! Attentive and experienced staff! Lunch options are great, with a wide variety of noodle and rice dishes, as well as soups. A few worthwhile house specials as well!
